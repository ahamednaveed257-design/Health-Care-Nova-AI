## Style Prompt

Care Nova AI tutorial video: clinical, premium, calm, and practical. The look should feel like a polished healthcare command center built for patients, doctors, and hackathon judges. Use a light medical workspace with navy authority, teal safety accents, and blue intelligence highlights. Motion should feel precise and supportive, not flashy.

- Background: #f4fbfc
- Surface: #ffffff
- Ink: #102033

- Mint: #e7fbf6

## Typography

- Display: Georgia, serif
- Body: Arial, sans-serif
- Data: Consolas, monospace

## Motion

- Use smooth clinical motion: slide, soft scale, and focus fades.
- Use teal and blue progress sweeps for transitions.
- Keep all text readable for a 1920 by 1080 explainer.
- Hold each chapter long enough for users to read the title, example, output, quality check, and next action.
- Use larger caption safe zones; do not place narration text over dense cards or progress labels.
- Keep motion purposeful: highlight where to look, then pause long enough for the user to understand the action.

## V24 Detail Rules

- Every chapter must show: use case, what to enter, what the agent checks, what output is produced, quality check, mastery tip, and next action.
- The Guide should teach real usage, not market the product.
- Visual labels must say V24 so the app, rendered video, and documentation match.
- Keep safety language clear: education, organization, and preparation only.

## What NOT To Do

- No dark neon theme.
- No tiny text or crowded tables.
- No unrelated medical claims.
- No diagnosis, prescription, dosage, or emergency automation wording.
- No jump cuts between scenes.
