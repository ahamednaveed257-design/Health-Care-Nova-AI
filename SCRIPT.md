# Care Nova AI Usage Tutorial Script

## Title

How to use Care Nova AI clearly and efficiently.

## Improved V24 22-Chapter Flow

Each chapter is intentionally paced with larger text, slower screen changes, clearer examples, a repeatable "what to enter, what Care Nova checks, what you receive" pattern, all three core teaching points, one mastery tip, one quality check, and one next action that explains what the user should save, verify, download, or escalate.

1. What Care Nova is: a local-first health agent workspace for questions, records, vitals, medicines, labs, visits, safety, and insurance.
2. Privacy setup: local app server storage, local OneDrive mirror, and no automatic cloud upload.
3. Profile: age, conditions, medicines, allergies, baseline readings, doctor, caregiver, and emergency contact.
4. Best input: main concern, timing, severity, readings, medicine timing, and warning signs.
5. Agentic flow: memory store, intent route, selected agent, safety guardrails, response, and memory update.
6. General: first-layer health Q&A for symptoms, prevention, and safe next steps.
7. Specialist: focused disease review for core body systems and chronic conditions.
8. Atlas: professional local health library for disease, medicine, lab, prevention, imaging, first-aid, warning-sign, and safety-boundary guides.
9. Vitals: BP, pulse, oxygen, sugar, temperature, BMI, hydration, activity, trend review, maintenance tips, and measurement quality.
10. Medicine: generic/branded medicines, uses, side effects, missed timing, interactions, allergy signs, storage, and pharmacist questions.
11. Labs: upload or paste report data, understand values, check trends, identify missing context, and download patient summaries.
12. Wellness: age-group healthy-life plans, sleep, diet, hydration, walking, stress, mental wellness, and habit feedback.
13. Visits: booking preparation, follow-up notes, doctor questions, urgency, specialty choice, and visit handoff.
14. Safety: urgent warning signs, why flagged, first safe action, what to collect, and escalation boundaries.
15. Insurance: claims, coverage, documents, prior authorization, EOB review, benefits, appeals, and plan-fit support.
16. Records: patient history, local storage, OneDrive mirror, search, type grouping, and patient-specific report export.
17. Summary: what happened till now, doctor-ready note, risk trend, records quality, missing context, and readiness score.
18. Modes and language: visible difference between Guided, Expert, and interface language selection.
19. Online and offline: offline continuity plus optional approved-source online enrichment and local cache.
20. Accuracy loop: save answers, approve feedback, mark missing context, and improve future routing behavior without changing governed medical facts.
21. Best workflow: profile, choose tab, ask one question, review safety, save output, update records, and open Summary.
22. Safe close: education and organization only; no diagnosis, prescriptions, dosage, or emergency automation.

## V24 Information Upgrade

- Shows all three teaching points for every chapter instead of only two.
- Adds a next-action ribbon so users know exactly what to save, verify, share, download, or escalate after each tab.
- Uses 14.8-second chapters, a larger subtitle area, cleaner captions, a structured bottom action tray, stronger chapter labels, and a higher-quality WebM export target.
- Keeps the 22-chapter structure and 1440p readable layout while improving the practical operating instructions.

## Safety Line

Care Nova AI supports education, organization, and safe next-step guidance. It does not diagnose, prescribe, calculate personal dosage, call emergency services, or replace professional medical care.
